Quotebook

About This Piece

This project was a quotebook that we needed to design and the subject of the quotebook was up to us to decide on. I ended up doing travel quotes, and I made each initial capital represent a different part of the world. The first page is a compass that I hand drew and each inital capital is hand drawn however the quotes themselves were printed. I used tea bags to stain the pages and make them look old then I wrapped the book in packaging paper and made a fake wax stamp to maje it look like an old fashioned parcel.
